History of the St. Bernard

The St.Bernard got their name from a dangerous past in the ALPS called st.bernard pass.The breed is most known for rescuing people who were trapped in the snow and in avalanches. St.bernards are very large muscular dogs they can weigh between 140 and 180 pounds St.bernards usually do not reach their full size untilthey are 2 or 3 years old.
